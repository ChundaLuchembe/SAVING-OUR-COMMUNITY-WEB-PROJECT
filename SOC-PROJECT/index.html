About Us

SOC is a non-governmental organization focused on bridging gaps in economic, health, agriculture, and education sectors for vulnerable groups in Livingstone, Zambia.

SOC was established in 2023 and registered as an NGO in 2024. The organization focuses on empowering vulnerable groups, including women, youths, and orphaned children. Our key activities include:

  • Providing educational scholarships and mentorship programs.
  • Promoting health awareness and sanitation through WASH initiatives.
  • Engaging in environmental and wildlife conservation efforts.
  • Supporting youth development and capacity-building programs.
