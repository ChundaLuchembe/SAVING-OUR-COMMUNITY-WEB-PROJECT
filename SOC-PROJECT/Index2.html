About Us

Godspeed is a non-governmental organization founded to bridge gaps in economic, health, agriculture, and education sectors. We empower women, youths, and vulnerable children in Livingstone, Zambia.

Our Programs

Education

Scholarships and mentorship programs for children and youths.

Health

Programs on reproductive health, HIV/AIDS prevention, and mental health awareness.

Agriculture

Farmer training, financial literacy, and vocational skills development.

Environment

Climate change awareness and wildlife protection initiatives.
